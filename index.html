About See Through Facades

On Target

Working with you

Where you need us



Founded in 2018, See Through Facades is a glass and aluminium contractor involved in the installation of glass and aluminium across Queensland. 

The See Through Facades team’s focus is on creating a business that offers impeccable service to the construction industry, supported by strong project management skills, with reliable on-site installation. These goals will be achieved by the establishment of open and clear client communication while also working in a cooperative nature to achieve project goals.

Due to our team’s extensive experience and contacts within the glass and aluminium industry, which has been gained via the management of multi-million dollar glazing contracts, See Through Facades are in the unique position to undertake any size contract.

Through a combination of local and international partners See Through Facades has the ability to fabricate commercial aluminium products in Rockhampton or Brisbane, or to import Australian designed and supported products from the international market.

Our Capabilities.

See Through Facades has the experience and expertise to supply and install any type of glass or aluminium shopfront. Our team’s experience is extensive and below are some of the services we offer.

Aluminium Windows and Doors
Internal Glazing to Partitions
Shop Front Facades
Frameless Glass Wall Structures and Spider Fittings
High Rise Window Frames
Curtain Wall
Glass Awnings

Have a Vision?

Quality Assurance

See Through Facades provides products and services to the building and construction industries in Queensland. Our intention is to maintain the highest level of quality and reliability at the lowest practicable cost as expected by our customers. We aim to surpass customer expectations in areas of response, product, workmanship and delivery together with cost effectiveness.

We will maintain our reputation for quality by continually achieving standards which will comply with all specifications as well as legal and regulatory requirements. See Through Facades management has put into place for all employees procedures and systems to cover Workplace Health & Safety, Environment and Quality Assurance management via our IT system. 

Environmental Management 

It is the policy of See Through Facades to achieve a high standard of environmental care in conducting its business. This commitment is reflected in the planning and performance of activities to ensure that adverse effects on the environment are either avoided or minimised. We seek to work with stakeholders to reduce the impact of waste where possible, also through re-cycling and re-use systems.
