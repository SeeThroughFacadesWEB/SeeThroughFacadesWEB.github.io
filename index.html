Founded in 2018, See Through Facades is a glass and aluminium contractor involved in the installation of glass and aluminium across Queensland. The See Through Facades team’s focus is on creating a business that offers impeccable service to the construction industry, supported by strong project management skills, with reliable on-site installation. These goals will be achieved by the establishment of
open and clear client communication while also working in a cooperative nature to achieve project goals. 

Easy and Simple to Use

Due to our team’s extensive experience and contacts within the glass and aluminium industry, which has been gained via the management of multi-million dollar glazing contracts, See Through Facades are in the unique position to undertake any size contract.

Through a combination of local and international partners See Through Facades has the ability to fabricate commercial aluminium products in Rockhampton or Brisbane, or to import Australian designed and supported products from the international market.
